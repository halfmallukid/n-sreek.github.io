    Section1:How to make an old fashioned in the right order

    1. Get a glass
    2. Add half a teaspoon of sugar or break a sugar cube
    3. Add some bitters, preferably, an angostura bitter
    4. Add some water and mix thoroughly
    5. Add 30-40ml of Rye Whisky
    6. Add ice cubes but do not dilute
    7. Twist the zest of a thin orange peel into the glass
      1. Cut a thin spiral strip of orange peel by peeling slowly around the orange
      2. Twist the orange peel so that the zest comes out
      3. Leave the beautifully <twisted> orange peel in the glass
